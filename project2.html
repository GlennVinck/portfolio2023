Yogism

Yogism aims to support users to perform yoga trough AI-driven coaching, ensuring correct posture and technique, and reducing the risk of injuries or discomfort. It provides personalized exercises for users of all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Users can create and save their own routines, share them, and exchange photos and videos of their poses with a community. The app places a strong emphasis on inclusivity, focusing on the health and well-being of all users, regardless of gender, age, weight, or personal characteristics.
