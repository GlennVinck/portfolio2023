HoloHand Museum Guide

Museum Dr. Guislain in Ghent is looking for a digital product (application) that enhances, improves, and/or supports the museum experience. The goal is to identify existing pain points within the museum and devise a unique digital solution for them. The challenge is to brainstorm various solutions and create a concept, which does not necessarily need to be feasible.

The Solution

Let yourself be guided through the museum by Dr. Guislain himself, a small hologram on your arm connected to an application on your smartphone. With the app, you can fully immerse yourself in the history and environment through augmented reality. You can also receive more information about what you see or relive your personal visit afterward.
